Hi, I'm Leanna Yue!

“Design is where empathy meets inquiry—where we shape not only how things work, but how they matter.” I design and research to create thoughtful, human-centered technologies grounded in usability, emerging tech, and sustainable futures.

I’m Le (Leanna) Yue, a soon-to-be graduate with a Master’s in Interaction Design from the University of Sydney, working at the intersection of UX research, speculative design, and material exploration. I’ve collaborated with Dr. Marius Hoggenmueller, Dr. Tram Nguyen, and Xinyan Xu on autonomous vehicle-pedestrian interaction projects, and with Dr. Wenye Li and Vincent Pham on sustainable lighting design using bio-based materials.

With a background in Architecture and experience as a UX Designer at Tesla, I combine spatial thinking, data-driven research, and storytelling to craft interactive experiences. My interests span AR/VR, AI-assisted design, wearable tech, and eco-conscious interaction design.

I’m open to collaboration, full-time opportunities, and creative partnerships—feel free to reach out via email or Linkedin!
